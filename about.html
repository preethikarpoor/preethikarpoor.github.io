About

Hello and welcome! I am Preethi R. Karpoor, a second-year Astronomy Graduate Student at the University of California, San Diego. I am an Observational Astronomer (in the making) and my research focuses on investigating exoplanet demographics around low-mass stars using data from the Transiting Exoplanet Survey Satellite (TESS). I am a member of the Stellar Data Lab and the Cool Stars Lab at UC San Diego, where I work under the guidance of Prof. Christopher Theissen, Assistant Professor in the Department of Astronomy and Astrophysics

I was born and raised in the bustling Silicon Valley of India, Bangalore. I completed my Bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering in 2020. My passion for astronomy has been a lifelong journey, and I have been involved in various projects since the beginning of my undergraduate studies.
