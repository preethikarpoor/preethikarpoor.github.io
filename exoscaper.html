EXOSCAPER

EXOSCAPER (Exoplanet Survey and Characterization Program for Earth-like Rocky planets) is a pipeline that has been developed to detect and characterize terrestrial planets around low-mass stars. This pipeline leverages the Transit Least Squares (TLS) algorithm for planet detection, which is particularly effective for identifying transits in light curves with high noise levels. EXOSCAPER integrates multi-planet detection, statistical vetting, and stellar activity measurement to provide a comprehensive analysis of potential exoplanet candidates.
